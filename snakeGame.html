    Snake Game

    Score: 0 High Score: 0

    Rules

    • The player controls a long, thin creature, resembling a snake, which roams around on a bordered plane, picking up food (or some other item), trying to avoid hitting its own tail or the edges of the playing area.

    • Each time the snake eats a piece of food, its tail grows longer, making the game increasingly difficult.

    • Player's Highest Score and Current Score are diplayed on top of the playing area.

    • Play the game with arrow keys of your keyboard or the arrow buttons below the playing area. We recommend the former method.
